# HyperSurvivor Narrative Production Master Plan

Date: 2026-04-03
Project: `/home/othman/Lenovo-Workspace/01-projets-actifs/hypersurvivor`

## Goal

Deliver a version of HyperSurvivor that feels unmistakably premium within 7 days.

The player must immediately understand:
- Jeff is the main character.
- The world is not a random crypto backdrop; it is a mythic Hyperliquid ecosystem.
- The 10 bosses are not generic enemies; they are recognizable stylized avatars of real ecosystem energy.
- Every run feels like a journey through belief, hype, prestige, collapse, hidden structure, and power.

This plan prioritizes visible impact over invisible polish.

## Creative Rules

### Jeff First

Jeff is the emotional anchor of the game.

Rules:
- Every major cinematic starts from Jeff's point of view or clearly frames Jeff against the domain he is entering.
- Jeff is the survivor, not the ruler.
- Jeff is not a meme. He is the only grounded human thread inside a mythologized market-system.

### Boss Framing

The 10 bosses already exist in code and remain the canonical gameplay bosses:
- `murad`
- `carlos`
- `ape`
- `dokwon`
- `logan`
- `ruja`
- `caroline`
- `kwon`
- `sam`
- `cz`

Rules:
- Bosses stay recognizable as figures inspired by real people or real ecosystem energy.
- They should be stylized, mythologized, and dramatized, not treated like flat documentary portraits.
- We keep the recognition signal, but push them into archetype territory.

### Tone

The tone is:
- mythic
- dystopian
- financial
- cultic
- neon
- tragic but not nihilistic

Avoid:
- irony overload
- parody-only framing
- generic "crypto montage" with no narrative spine

## Narrative Spine

### Core Premise

The market is no longer a place. It became a world.

Jeff enters that world as a survivor, not a king. Each boss represents a force that now rules one part of this ecosystem:
- belief
- noise
- herd violence
- prestige
- clout
- illusion
- calculation
- collapse
- hidden architecture
- sovereignty

### 4-Act Structure

#### Act 1: Belief Becomes Infrastructure
- Murad
- Carlos
- Ape

Jeff learns that conviction, hype, and collective force can become physical systems.

#### Act 2: Image Becomes Power
- Sun King
- Logan
- Ruja

Jeff enters territories where prestige, spectacle, and illusion dominate reality.

#### Act 3: Structure Becomes Predation
- Caroline
- Do Kwon
- Sam

Jeff confronts accounting, collapse, hidden doors, and engineered fragility.

#### Act 4: Power Becomes Order
- CZ

Jeff faces the final form of the system: empire, force, and dominance.

## Opening Cinematic

### Purpose

Introduce:
- Jeff
- the world
- the 10 forces
- the idea that survival means walking through every face of power

### Duration

Target: `45s to 60s`

### Script Beats

1. The market-city reveals itself as a living world.
2. Jeff appears alone inside it.
3. The ecosystem is shown as a hierarchy of rulers, ghosts, prophets, and operators.
4. The 10 bosses are teased as domains.
5. Jeff accepts the crossing.
6. Title card lands.

### Final Line

`Enter the system.`

## Acceptance Criteria

The build is successful if:
- the player immediately understands Jeff is the lead
- each boss feels like a distinct recognizable figure
- every boss arrival feels premium
- opening, victory, and defeat all feel authored
- audio identity is clear and not generic
- no broken media flow blocks a run

## Non-Goals For This 7-Day Window

Do not spend the week on:
- adaptive music architecture that is too complex
- long cutscenes for every stage
- perfect lip sync
- a huge lore codex inside the game
- overly complex asset pipeline refactors
